Adding a child to your bank account can seem practical, but it can have legal and financial consequences.

Joint accounts may give the other person ownership rights, which can affect how funds are treated after death or if disputes arise.

There are other ways to allow someone to help manage finances without changing ownership.

Understanding the difference helps avoid outcomes you did not intend.
