<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>Assets held in trust by a trustee for the benefit of named beneficiaries. While colloquially referring to financial assets, legally a trust fund encompasses all property transferred to and held by a trust, including real estate, securities, business interests, or any other property.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
