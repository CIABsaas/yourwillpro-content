<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A written legal document specifying what medical treatments a person wants or does not want if they become unable to communicate their wishes. Also called an advance directive or advance care directive.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
