  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A person's mother or father. In legal contexts, particularly inheritance and family law, "parent" typically includes biological parents and adoptive parents, but generally excludes step-parents unless specifically stated otherwise or they've legally adopted the child. For intestacy and family provision claims, parents are a defined class with specific rights.</p>
