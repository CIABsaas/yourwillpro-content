<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The amount payable from a superannuation fund upon a member's death, distributed according to binding death benefit nominations, non-binding nominations, or trustee discretion as governed by superannuation law and the fund's trust deed. Death benefits can be paid as lump sums or pensions to eligible beneficiaries, subject to specific tax treatment.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>

Who receives it depends on whether you've made a valid binding death benefit nomination, otherwise the super fund trustee decides. It doesn't automatically go through your willâ€”super is separate.</p>
