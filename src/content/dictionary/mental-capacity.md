<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The legal ability of a person to understand information relevant to a decision and to appreciate the consequences of making or not making that decision. Required capacity varies by decision type and can fluctuate over time.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>Your ability to understand what you're doing and make decisions for yourself. It's not about whether you make wise decisions others agree with‚Äîit's about whether you understand what you're deciding and the consequences.</p>
  </div>
