<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A self-managed superannuation fund is a private superannuation trust structure regulated by the Australian Taxation Office, with members acting as trustees who have direct control over investment decisions and fund management. SMSFs must comply with strict superannuation and taxation laws, and are subject to annual audits and regulatory reporting.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>An SMSF is a do-it-yourself super fund where you're in control instead of a bank or super company. You and up to five other members manage the investments yourselves, deciding where the money goes. With control comes responsibilityâ€”you must follow strict rules and handle all the paperwork and compliance.</p>
