<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A fiduciary who holds legal title to trust property and is bound by law to manage that property solely for the benefit of the trust's beneficiaries according to the trust's terms. The trustee owes duties of loyalty, care, impartiality, and prudence, and must administer the trust in good faith.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
