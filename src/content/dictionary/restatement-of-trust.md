<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A comprehensive revision of a revocable trust that restates all trust terms from the beginning while maintaining the original trust's existence and tax identification number. Unlike an amendment which modifies specific provisions, a restatement replaces the entire trust document with updated terms while keeping the same trust entity.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>A restatement is like rewriting your trust from scratch while keeping the same trust legally in place. Instead of making lots of individual amendments, you create a whole new document that replaces everything in the original trust. The trust itself doesn't endâ€”you're just completely updating its instructions.</p>
