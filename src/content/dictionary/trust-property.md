<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>All property, assets, and interests legally transferred to and held by a trust, owned by the trustee in their fiduciary capacity for the benefit of the trust's beneficiaries. Trust property can include real estate, financial accounts, securities, business interests, intellectual property, or any other transferable asset.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>Trust property is everything actually owned by the trustâ€”your house if you transferred it in, your investments if they're titled to the trust, any property formally moved into the trust's name. If it's not been transferred to the trust, it's not trust property.</p>
