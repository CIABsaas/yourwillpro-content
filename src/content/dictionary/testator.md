<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A person who executes a will or dies having left a valid will. The testator is the individual whose testamentary wishes are expressed in the will document, and who had testamentary capacity and intent when creating it. Traditionally gender-neutral, though "testatrix" was historically used for women.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
