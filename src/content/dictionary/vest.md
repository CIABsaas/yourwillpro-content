<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>To confer an immediate, fixed right to present or future property ownership that cannot be taken away, even though actual possession or enjoyment may be postponed. Once an interest vests, the holder has a legally protected right to the property, though conditions may delay when they receive it.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>When property rights "vest," you get a guaranteed right to it even if you can't have it yet. Like a trust saying you get the money at age 25â€”your right vests immediately, but possession vests (you actually get it) at 25.</p>
  </div>
