<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>Completely lacking in legal force or effect from inception; null and unenforceable as if it never existed. A void document, will, or transaction is invalid ab initio (from the beginning) and cannot be validated or ratified, regardless of subsequent events or parties' intentions.</p>
  </div>
