<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A will that satisfies all statutory requirements for execution, including testamentary capacity, testamentary intent, proper form, and compliance with witnessing and signature formalities as prescribed by the governing jurisdiction's law. A valid will can be admitted to probate and enforced according to its terms.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
