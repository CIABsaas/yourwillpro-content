<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>An individual or institution designated in a trust document to assume the role of trustee upon the death, resignation, incapacity, or removal of the original or current trustee. The successor trustee steps into all duties and powers of the former trustee, ensuring continuity of trust administration.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
