    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A will made orally (spoken aloud) rather than in writing, typically made in contemplation of imminent death. Also called an oral will or deathbed will. Now either completely invalid or valid only in extremely limited circumstances in most jurisdictions (military personnel on active service, mariners at sea, with multiple witnesses required).</p>
