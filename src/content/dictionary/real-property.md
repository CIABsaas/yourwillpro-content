<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>Land and all permanent improvements or structures affixed to the land, including buildings, fences, and fixtures. Real property is immovable and transfers through deeds with specific legal formalities, distinct from personal property which is movable.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>Real property is land and anything permanently attached to it‚Äîyour house, your garage, a fence, landscaping. Basically, if you can't pick it up and move it, it's real property. Everything else (furniture, cars, jewelry) is personal property.</p>
  </div>
</div>
