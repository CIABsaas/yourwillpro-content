<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A person's closest living blood relatives, as determined by law. In intestacy, next of kin inherit when someone dies without a will, following statutory rules of priority (spouse, children, parents, siblings). In medical contexts, refers to the person authorized to make decisions for an incapacitated person.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>

Important: next of kin is determined by law, not personal choice‚Äîyou can't just designate anyone, though you can appoint specific people through wills and powers of attorney.</p>
