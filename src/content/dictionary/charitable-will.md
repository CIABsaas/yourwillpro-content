<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A will that includes one or more bequests to registered charitable organizations or not-for-profit entities. May be wholly charitable (entire estate to charity) or mixed (some to charity, some to family). Can provide tax benefits in certain jurisdictions.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
