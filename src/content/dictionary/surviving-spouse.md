<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The person who was legally married to a decedent at the time of death and who outlives the decedent. Surviving spouses typically have special legal rights including elective share rights, family allowances, homestead protections, and preferential inheritance treatment.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>Your surviving spouse is your husband or wife who's still alive after you die. The law gives surviving spouses special protections‚Äîthey can't usually be completely disinherited and often get tax advantages other beneficiaries don't receive.</p>
