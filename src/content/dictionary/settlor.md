<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The person who creates a trust by transferring property to a trustee to hold for the benefit of beneficiaries. Also called grantor (US term) or trustor. The settlor establishes the trust's terms, determines beneficiaries, and specifies how the trust property should be managed and distributed.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>The settlor is the person who sets up a trust. If you create a trust and put your money or property into it, you're the settlor. You decide who the beneficiaries are, who manages it (the trustee), and what rules they must follow.</p>
  </div>
</div>
