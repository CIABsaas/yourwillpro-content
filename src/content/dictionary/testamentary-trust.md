<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A trust established through provisions in a will that takes effect upon the testator's death. The trust does not exist during the testator's lifetime and is created only when the will is probated. Commonly used for minor children, beneficiaries with special needs, or asset protection purposes.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>A testamentary trust is a trust you create in your will that springs into existence when you die. During your life, it's just words in your willâ€”the trust doesn't actually exist until after your death when the will goes through probate.</p>
  </div>
