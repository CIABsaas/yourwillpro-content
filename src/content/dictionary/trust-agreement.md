<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The foundational legal document establishing a trust, defining the settlor's intent, naming trustees and beneficiaries, specifying trust property, and setting forth the terms governing trust administration and distribution. Also called a trust document, trust instrument, or declaration of trust.</p>
