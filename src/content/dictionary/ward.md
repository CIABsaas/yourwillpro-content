<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>An individual, usually a minor child or incapacitated adult, placed under the protection and authority of a court-appointed guardian. The ward lacks full legal capacity to manage their own affairs, and the guardian is responsible for their personal care, property management, or both, as determined by the court.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>A ward is someone under the legal care of a guardian‚Äîusually a child whose parents died or can't care for them, or an incapacitated adult who can't manage their own affairs. The guardian makes decisions for the ward.</p>
  </div>
</div>
