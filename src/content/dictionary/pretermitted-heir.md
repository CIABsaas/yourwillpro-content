    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>

    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>

Most common example: children born after you made your will. But some jurisdictions also protect other family members accidentally omitted. The law steps in to give these "forgotten" heirs a share of your estate, usually what they would've gotten if you'd died without a will.</p>
