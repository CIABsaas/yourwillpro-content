<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The person who creates a trust by transferring property to a trustee for the benefit of beneficiaries. Synonymous with settlor or grantor, the trustor establishes the trust's terms, funds it with property, and determines how it will operate. Commonly used in some jurisdictions, particularly in the western United States.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
