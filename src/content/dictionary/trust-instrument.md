<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The written legal document establishing a trust and defining its terms, provisions, and administration. The trust instrument sets forth the settlor's intentions, identifies parties (settlor, trustee, beneficiaries), describes trust property, grants powers, imposes duties, and establishes distribution terms. Synonymous with trust document or trust agreement.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
