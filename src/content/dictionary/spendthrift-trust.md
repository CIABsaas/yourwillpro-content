<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A trust containing a spendthrift clause that restricts the beneficiary's ability to transfer their interest and shields trust assets from the beneficiary's creditors. The trustee has discretion over distributions, protecting against improvident beneficiaries who might waste the inheritance or have it seized by creditors.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>A spendthrift trust protects inheritance from irresponsible beneficiaries and their creditors. The trustee controls when and how much money is distributed, so a spendthrift beneficiary can't blow through their inheritance immediately or have creditors seize it before they receive it.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="example-box">
  <div class="example-label">EXAMPLE</div>
  <p>"Grandma set up a spendthrift trust for my cousin who has struggled with addiction and debt. The trustee pays his rent and necessities directly, but my cousin can't demand lump sums or use his future inheritance as collateral for loans. It keeps him housed and fed without enabling his problems."</p>
