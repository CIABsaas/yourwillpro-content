<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A fiduciary relationship in which a trustee holds legal title to property for the benefit of one or more beneficiaries. Created by a settlor who transfers property to the trustee, trusts separate legal ownership from beneficial ownership, imposing strict fiduciary duties on the trustee to manage trust property for the beneficiaries' benefit according to the trust's terms.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>A trust is a legal arrangement where you give property to someone (the trustee) to hold and manage for someone else's benefit (the beneficiaries). The trustee legally owns it but must use it only for the beneficiaries according to the trust's rules.</p>
  </div>
</div>
