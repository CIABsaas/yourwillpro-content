<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A beneficiary named in a will to receive the residue or remainder of an estate after all specific bequests, debts, taxes, and administration expenses have been satisfied. The residuary beneficiary takes what's left over, which may be the bulk of the estate or nothing if debts and expenses consume everything.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>The residuary beneficiary gets "everything else" after specific gifts are made and bills are paid. If your will says "I give $10,000 to my friend and the rest to my daughter," your daughter is the residuary beneficiaryâ€”she gets whatever remains after that $10,000 and all debts are paid.</p>
  </div>
