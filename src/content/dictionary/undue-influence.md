<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>Excessive persuasion, pressure, or manipulation that overcomes the free will of a testator or settlor, causing them to execute a will or trust contrary to their true wishes. Undue influence requires showing the influencer had opportunity, motive, and actually exerted pressure that substituted their will for the testator's independent judgment.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>Undue influence is when someone pressures or manipulates you into making a will that reflects their wishes, not yours. It's more than just persuasion‚Äîit's crossing the line into coercion that overpowers your own free choice.</p>
  </div>
</div>
