<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>A will executed when the testator is gravely ill, dying, or facing imminent death. While legally valid if properly executed, deathbed wills face heightened scrutiny because the testator may be vulnerable to influence or lack capacity due to illness or medication.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>A will you make when you're dying‚Äîin hospital, a nursing home, or at home in your final days or weeks. These are legally valid but often challenged, especially if they change an earlier will or benefit caregivers.</p>
  </div>
</div>
