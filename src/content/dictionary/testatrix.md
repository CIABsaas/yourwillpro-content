<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>Historically, the feminine form of testator, referring to a female person who makes or has made a will. Modern legal practice has largely abandoned this gendered terminology in favor of the gender-neutral term "testator" for all will-makers regardless of gender.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
    <div class="column-label">PLAIN ENGLISH</div>
    <p>Testatrix is an old-fashioned term for a woman who made a will. You'll see it in older wills and legal documents, but today we just use "testator" for everyone, regardless of gender.</p>
  </div>
