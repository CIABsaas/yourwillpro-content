<div class="two-column">
  <div class="column-card legal-column">
    <div class="column-label">LEGAL DEFINITION</div>
    <p>The aggregate of all property, assets, and interests held in a trust at any particular time. The trust estate includes the original property transferred to the trust (corpus or principal), plus any income, appreciation, or property acquired by the trust, minus any distributions or expenses.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="column-card plain-column">
